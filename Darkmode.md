## Interactive Elements (Buttons & UI)

### Orange Theme (Hire Me / Footer)

- **Gradient:** `#fb923c` to `#ea580c`
- **Border:** `#f97316`
- **Shadows:** `#c2410c`, `#9a3412`
- **Text:** `#7a2800`

### Blue Theme (Email / About Me)

- **Gradient:** `#5a7ff7` to `#3d62f0`
- **Border:** `#2f50e0`
- **Shadows:** `#1a35a8`, `#122890`
- **Text:** `#f0f4ff`

### Green Theme (Availability / About Me)

- **Gradient:** `#6ef782` to `#58f46e`
- **Border:** `#40d855`
- **Shadows:** `#28a83d`, `#167a26`
- **Text:** `#0a3a12`

### Pink Theme (Resume / Career)

- **Gradient:** `#f780b2` to `#ee5a99`
- **Border:** `#d4478a`
- **Shadows:** `#a32460`, `#8a1a50`
- **Text:** `#3a0d20`
